What is ScreenReps?

ScreenReps is a digital wellness app for iOS. You pick the apps that distract you most — Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, whatever it is — and ScreenReps shields them when your daily limit runs out.

To earn more time, you do real exercise: pushups, squats, plank holds, walking, or a check-in at your gym. Reps become minutes. Movement becomes scrolling. Your phone stops being free.

How it works

1

Pick what to block

Choose the apps and websites that drain your time. Set a daily allowance.

2

Pick how you move

Pushups, squats, plank, walking, or gym check-ins. Set your own rep-to-minute ratio.

3

Earn it back

Complete the exercise, unlock the apps. Banked minutes carry over throughout the day.

Privacy first. No exceptions.

ScreenReps is built around a simple idea: your data should belong to you. We don't have servers, we don't have accounts, we don't track you.
